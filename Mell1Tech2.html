The Power of Owning the Entire Solution

The price for a Mell1Tech2 solution will pay for itself since there are no continual expense once the solution has been developed.

Solutions can range from one off additions to website to a full login that requires a longer development time and even then, once developed it will be handed over to the business owner.

These solutions will be independent from the web builder and only require updates when the base programming language is updated.  All the documentation that went into producing the solutions will also be handed over so that any other developer can takeover.
